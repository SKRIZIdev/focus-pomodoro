About

The Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused 25-minute sprints separated by short breaks. After four sprints, take a longer break. It keeps your mind fresh and progress steady.

How to use FocusFlow

Pick a mode, hit start, and work until the timer rings. Track what you're doing in the task list, and check the stats page to see your streak of focus sessions.

Runs entirely in your browser — sessions are counted in localStorage.
